The Browse by Area of Interest page allows you to review volunteer opportunities that have been categorized under a Specific Area of Interest. This page lists all Areas of Interest beginning with the letter chosen from the "Select a Letter" menu. It is also possible to list all Areas alphabetically on a single page using the "Show All" link.

At the end of each Area of Interest, the number of records in the current View that contain that term is identified in brackets. Clicking on a specific Area of Interest name produces a list of current Opportunities in your View that contain that Area of Interest.

If the message that appears on this page is: "Found 0 matches", then there are no Areas of Interest in the database that begin with the letter chosen. In this case, use the "Select a Letter" menu to choose a different starting letter. Alternatively, return to the main menu using the "New Search" button or your browser's back button and begin a new search.

A Super User can add to the list of Areas of Interest by going to the Checklists > Specific Areas of Interest. It is important to make sure that all Volunteer Opportunity records are assigned to appropriate Areas of Interest so they can be found in searches.
