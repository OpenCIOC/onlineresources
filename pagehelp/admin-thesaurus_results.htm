Changing the Results Display

If you wish to change the display of your search results, you must return to the search form. At the bottom of the search form, you will see options for changing the display of your search results.

You may sort the list of terms by the Name of the Term (English or French, depending on your current display language), or by the Last Modified date of the term with most recently modified Terms appearing first.

By default, the results display will include:

  • The Term name (in the current language)
  • The preferred Term (if the term is Unused, which Term to use instead)
  • The number of records using the subject. This count varies depending on your permissions and whether you work in a database with other CIOC Members
    • In a single-member database, the count includes all records in all Views.
    • In a multi-member database if you are a Local Super User, you will see a count only for records owned by your CIOC Membership. This will not include records that have been shared with you.
    • In a multi-member database if you are a Global Super User, you will see a count for your own records in brackets (#) as well as a count in square brackets for all other records in the database owned by other CIOC Members [+#].

By selected Display Full Subject Information, you will see all available information about the subject including:

  • Broader, Narrower and Related Terms
  • Used For information - where the given Term is a preferred Term for another, Unused Term.
  • Data management information.
