The "Download" page provides automatically generated links to the most recent Microsoft Access download for your database. There is one download page per software module (Community Information or Volunteer), and the page is accessible only be Super Users in that module.

Adding and Removing Additional Resources

In addition to the automatically generated links, you may add your own list of links to the Download page. Links may be added in one or more of the languages available in your database. To remove a custom link, click the delete button next to the link.
