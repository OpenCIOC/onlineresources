The "Confirm Delete Feedback" page allows you to confirm your intention to delete feedback submitted or to delete a request for a new record.

Clicking the "Delete Feedback" button will permanently remove any feedback or suggestions for a new record. If you decide you do not want to delete the information, use your browser's back button or the Feedback link in your menu to return to the previous page. Before deleting, you may want to email or phone the submitter if appropriate to inform them of why their suggestions are not being processed.

Warning: use this option with caution; once deleted, the information cannot be retrieved. You may want to archive your feedback emails so that you have record to refer back to if necessary.
