The Record Inclusion Policy page is where information is entered to display to users who are at the "Suggest a New Record" page of the database (feedback.asp). The text entered here opens in a pop-up window when the "Record Inclusion Policy" link at the top of feedback.asp is clicked. Once you have created Inclusion Policies for your database, you will be able to select a policy for each language for use in a given View from within the View setup page. You may create multiple policies, or have just one policy for all modules (Community Information and Volunteer) and Views. Inclusion Policies are language-specific.

If there is no Inclusion Policy text, no link will be provided on the form. It is strongly recommended that you have at least a basic Inclusion Policy available if you intend to provide people with a link to suggest new records.

Clicking the "View the current Inclusion Policy page" link will display the policy currently saved to the database in a pop-up window. Note that your changes will not be reflected until you click the "Submit Updates" button at the bottom of the form to save your changes.

Inclusion Policy text can include HTML formatting and links. For example, you may want to add all of your inclusion information to appear within the pop-up window itself or you may prefer (particularly if you have a lengthy inclusion criteria document) to provide a link to an existing inclusion policy at another location. If you want your inclusion policy page to link to a file on another site, you may want it to open within a new full-size window; if so, use the target="_blank" attribute within the <a href="..."> tag you use to create your link.
