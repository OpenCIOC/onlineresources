New Reminder

The Reminders system is intended to assist users in creating task lists for themselves and other users, as well as provide a means of communicating information to other CIOC users. Reminders can be for general tasks or communications, or they may be tied to one or more records. You can work with reminders by clicking the Reminders link in your site bar, or (in the case of Reminders tied to specific records), through the Record Details page. You can also create a reminder for a group of records through your search results. Click the "Next" button to begin creating a new reminder for the selected records.

Print List

If your application has available at least one Print Profile available in your current View, you will be able to choose a specific Profile from the dropdown list on this page and begin preparing a customized print report. Print Profiles may be added or edited by a Super User through Setup > Print Profiles.

Email Update Request

This function allows you to prepare and send a bulk update email request to a selected group of records. Click the "Next" button to begin preparing an update email to be sent to the selected records.

Set Records Public / Non-Public

This function allows you to mark a group of records as Public or Non-Public. Select the desired option from the drop-down list and click"Next".

[ Top ]

Delete/Restore Records

This function will allow you to set or remove the deletion date on a group of records. The options available on this page from the dropdown list are "Set Deletion Date" (choose this to delete a group of records or change the Deletion Date of already deleted records) or "Restore Records" (choose this to remove deleted status from a group of records).

[ Top ]

Change Record Owner

This function allows you to change the Agency that is designated as the Record Owner for the selected records. Select the desired Agency from the drop-down list and click"Next". The list of Agencies available here is determined by which Agencies are marked as owning records in the current module (Community Information or Volunteer) in the Agency Setup area.

[ Top ]

Add/Remove Area of Interest

This will allow you to add or remove an Area of Interest from a set of records. If you group your Areas of Interest into broader categories, you will first need to select the general Area of Interest from the drop down menu and click "Submit". Choose either "Add" or "Delete" from the drop down menu, select the checkboxes beside the specific Area(s) of Interest you want to add or remove, and click "Submit". If you choose the "Delete All" option, you will be asked to check a box to confirm that you wish to remove every Area of Interest from the selected records.

[ Top ]

Add to Sharing Profile

When working in a multi-member database, this tool is used to add new records to an existing Sharing Profile to make the records available to other members in your database.
