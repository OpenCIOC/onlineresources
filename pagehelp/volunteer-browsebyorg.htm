The "Browse Organizations with Opportunities" page displays a list of organizations that have volunteer opportunities listed in this database. Click on a letter from the "Select a Letter" menu to view a list of all organizations whose name begins with that letter. It is also possible to list all organizations alphabetically on a single page using the "Show All" link.

The number in brackets following the name of the organization tells you how many opportunities the organization has available (note that this only counts Opportunities in your current View). To see a list of all opportunities available with a particular agency, click on the name of the organization.
