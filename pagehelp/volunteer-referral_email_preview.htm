For more information on Referral follow-up emails, view the help for the Customize Referral Follow-Up Email page.

The "Preview Referral Follow-Up Email" page allows you to preview the email message that you are about to send to one or more Organizations or potential Volunteers.

If you are sending a Referral follow-up email for more than one Referral request, your preview will be based on the first email in the queue.

If you are satisfied with the subject heading and body section of the email, click the "Send the Email Message" button at the bottom of the page to send out the email(s).

If you wish to make a change to the email after previewing it, use your browser's "back" button to return to the previous page where you can edit the email details.
